Recipe 1: Rigatoni Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb rigatoni pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook rigatoni according to package instructions.
  2. Heat marinara sauce in a pan.
  3. Drain pasta and add it to the sauce.
  4. Toss to coat and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Recipe 2: Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the olive oil mixture.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through.
  5. Serve with fresh lemon wedges.
  6. Enjoy your delicious Grilled Chicken!

Recipe 3: Vegetarian Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Cooked rice for serving
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and cornstarch to create the sauce.
  2. Stir-fry the mixed vegetables in a pan until slightly tender.
  3. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve over cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
  6. Enjoy your flavorful Vegetarian Stir-Fry!
